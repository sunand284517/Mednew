The Problem

Why MedAssist Now?

Unavailable During Night Hours

About Us

About MedAssist Now

Bridging the gap between patients and healthcare accessibility

Our Mission

MedAssist Now is a college project aimed at revolutionizing healthcare accessibility through technology. Our mission is to ensure that no patient has to struggle to find essential medicines, especially during emergencies.

What We Do

We provide a comprehensive platform that connects patients with pharmacies in real-time, enabling:

  • Instant medicine availability checks across multiple pharmacies
  • 24/7 emergency delivery services
  • Real-time order tracking from pharmacy to doorstep
  • Seamless coordination between patients, pharmacies, and delivery partners

Our Vision

To create a healthcare ecosystem where medicines are accessible to everyone, everywhere, anytime. This project demonstrates how technology can save lives by reducing the time between prescription and treatment.

For Patients

Quick access to medicine availability and emergency delivery services

For Pharmacies

Digital platform to manage inventory and reach more customers

For Delivery Partners

Flexible earning opportunities serving the healthcare sector
